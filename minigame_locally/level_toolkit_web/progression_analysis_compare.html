How the page decides
Reorder suggestions keep each level inside its current difficulty band. The curve shape stays intact, but levels can move earlier or later relative to peers with the same label. Suggestions use pair count, blocker load, board area, move pressure, solution count, and validation warnings to estimate whether a level feels light, middle, or heavy for its local band.
Tweak suggestions never ask for moves above the hard rule. They focus on slot fit, blocker pressure, endpoint isolation, dead-end risk, and whether the level looks too open or too compressed for its current placement.
